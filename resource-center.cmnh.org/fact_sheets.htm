  Diorama Fact Sheets

Dioramas are part of the Science Resource Center's loan program and can be borrowed by SRC members. (click here to join the SRC) The animals in the dioramas have died naturally or in accidents and have been taxidermically prepared by Museum staff. They are mounted in natural scenes. Many have a hands-on kit that can be borrowed with the diorama. All of the following fact sheets have been checked for accuracy. Each describes a unique diorama created by the Cleveland Museum of Natural History. To view the fact sheets you will need to to have Adobe Reader. Click here to download Adobe Reader .
