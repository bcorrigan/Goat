Loan Program

The Science Resource Center (SRC) invites teachers to take the Museum to their own schools by offering unique teaching materials which bring concepts to life for students. We offer over 4000 items for loan. Kits include specimens, artifacts, teacher guides, models, videos and much more. Dioramas are taxidermied animals in realistic settings. Included with all dioramas is an information sheet. Some dioramas now include a hands-on kit that includes an animal covering, skull, nonfiction books, tracks and molds. Click on the item below for a list of titles available. Reservations for kits, dioramas, videos, and slide sets are suggested. Membership to the SRC is required to borrow any material. (click here to join the SRC) Teachers (or their agents) must pick up and return all items. If you have any questions, please call (216)231-2075 or 231-4600 ext 3211.

Teachers Guides to student programs

The Museum provides teachers guides to all the student programming we offer whether its inside or outside Museum walls. All guides include the Ohio educational standard/indicator that will be addressed in the program along with vocabulary, pre-/post- activity suggestions, resources available from the Musuem for loan and more. Diorama Fact Sheets

Each diorama is accompanied by a free fact sheet. The sheet explains all the animals in the scene and includes information on the common and scientific name of each animal. Habitat, diet, nesting/breeding, and range information is also included. Click here to view dioramas and their fact sheets. You will need Adobe Reader to view the fact sheets.
