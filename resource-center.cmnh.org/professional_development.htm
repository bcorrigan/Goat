  Teacher Professional Development

The Science Resource Center offers many different levels of teacher professional development, from intensive graduate level college courses to more informal monthly workhshops. We offer classes to individual teachers or entire school staff. Please call (216) 231-4600 ext 3211 or email the SRC with your questions or to register.
