  Science-to-Go! Teacher's Guides

The Science-to-Go! is an outreach program that brings the Museum to your classroom. These one-hour, hands-on science presentations. Each presenation is designed to help your students meet the new Ohio Science Standards in grades K-8. Preschool presentations aim to expose younger students (ages 3-5) to nature through actual Museum specimens, models, and interactive games. To learn more about Science-to-Go! click here.
