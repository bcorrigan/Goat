module #Callvote# - Allows channel members to call public votes.
Based on Quake3 callvoting.
-----
#callvote <Question># - Starts a vote. Question should be a yes/no question.
Users can vote by saying, in the channel or a private message, one of the following:
(#yes#, #f1#, #ins#) | (#no#, #f2#, #del#)
These votes should be on their own on a line. Private messages will be replied to confirming vote cast.
#callvote kick <username># - calls a vote to kick a user (bot must be opped). Quorum of 3 yes votes required.
